What's IOTA?

IOTA, short for Icosahedron of Total Annihilation, is an object show that is currently being worked on by plz4x. (That's me!)
If you watch BFDI or II, you might know what these are.
Basically, object shows are a type of web series where a bunch of anthropomorphic objects battle for a prize, or go on adventures together. There are lots of them out there, and they're my favourite types of YouTube videos!
Here are some object shows that I like:
  • Battle for Dream Island
  • Inanimate Insanity
  • The Nightly Manor
  • Испытание На Миллион Тысяч (Contest for Million of Thousands. Russian is pronounced [ɪspytˈɑnʲijɪ nˈɑ mʲiɭɭʲiˈon tˈysʲʌt͡ʃʲ])
  • Animatic Battle
  • Love of the S*n ("S*n" is pronounced [sʌn].)
These shows are animated in Adobe Animate, but since I don't want to have to pay $22.99 a month just for my animations, I decided to draw them in comics instead, like Total Firey Island.
I will also be posting the comic pages on this website, for all to read.

The reason why it's called Icosahedron of Total Annihilation is because the prize is an icosahedron.
This icosahedron can do basically anything you want it to do, such as generate a million dollars, end world hunger, or completely annihilate the world. So yeah, basically, it's, like, really powerful.
